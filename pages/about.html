Our Story

Be the Underground.

Underground Garden is South Florida's home for underground electronic music — built by the community, for the community.

Community Over EverythingCommunity Over Everything

Where It Started

Built on rebellion, not permission.

Underground Garden started as a love for underground raves and a refusal to wait for someone else's invitation. Founders Chris and Marco turned that obsession with the culture into throwing their own events.

In February 2021, Underground Garden was born from that same energy: raw, community first, and unapologetically underground.

Today we run pool parties, beach parties, and kickass events across Miami and Fort Lauderdale. Every event building the community from the ground up.

Our Mission

Community over everything.

We're not here to throw the biggest party — we're here to build the strongest community. Every event is a chance to bring ravers, dancers, and music lovers together in a space that feels like ours.

The Garden Rules

What we stand for

Look Out for Each Other

We're a community built around inclusion. We're dedicated to creating a safe space, and that means we all look after each other as family.

All Are Welcome

Doesn't matter where you're from or what you look like. If you're here for the music, you're already family. Come as you are!

Give Back

Giving to the community is a promise we keep. We've hosted toy drives, food drives, animal fundraisers, and helped promote environmental foundations.

Signature Motif

Roots run deep.

The root and tree motif that runs through our merch and visuals ties "Garden" into everything we make — a symbol of a community that grows from the underground up.
