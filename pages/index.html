Our Story

From dive bars to independent festivals.

When the pandemic shut down the scene, we saw an empty dance floor and decided to fill it. Underground Garden started DIY. No big budget, just a love for the music and a belief that the culture deserved better. That belief turned into a community, and now we throw curated events across Miami and Fort Lauderdale.
